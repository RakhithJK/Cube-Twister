CubePlayer Applets

Read Me

With the CubePlayer applets you can integrate interactive Rubik's Cube-like puzzles into your HTML pages. The CubePlayer applets can play back cube scripts (aka maneuvers).

Note: The applet comes in a regular version and a 'flat' version. The 'flat' version needs much less memory than the regular version, but does not support a stickers image.
