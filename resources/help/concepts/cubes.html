Cubes

Rubik's Cubes come in various color schemes and shapes.

CubeTwister supports the following cube shapes:

  • 2x2 Pocket Cube
  • 3x3 Rubik's Cube
  • 3x3 Rubik's Barrel
  • 3x3 Rubik's Diamond
  • 3x3 Rubik's Octahedron
  • 4x4 Revenge Cube
  • 5x5 Professor Cube
  • 6x6 V-Cube 6
  • 6x6 Cube 6
  • 7x7 V-Cube 7
  • 7x7 Cube 7

You can customize the color schemes of the cubes. It's also possible to define disassembled cubes to show their mechanics.

The cubes are managed per document. To see the cubes in the current document open the folder named «Cubes» in the tree.
