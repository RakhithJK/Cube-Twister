Cube Parts

A Rubik's Cube has 6 faces. The cube is sliced into 3 layers on the x-axis, 3 layers on the y-axis and 3 layers on the z-axis.

A layer can have 4 different orientations. It can be turned by quarter turn twists and half turn twists. A quarter turn twist turns a layer by 90° in clockwise or in anticlockwise direction. A half turn twist turns a layer by 180°.

The cube has 6 side parts, 8 corner parts and 12 edge parts. There is also 1 center part, that keeps the cube together.

Side parts have 1 sticker, edge parts have 2 stickers, corner parts have 3 stickers.
