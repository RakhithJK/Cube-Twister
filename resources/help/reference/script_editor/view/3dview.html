The Script Editor - The 3D View

The 3D view shows an interactive view of a cube in three dimensions.

Please note: Changes made to the cube are not persistent. To make the state of the cube persistent you have to create a script by recording your twists or by using the built in solver.

To rotate the cube:

  1. Click with the mouse button on the background area of the 3D view.
  2. Move the mouse while keeping the mouse button pressed.

To twist the cube:

  1. Select the «Twist» tool in the toolbar.
  2. Click at a sticker of the cube.
    (This will twist one layer of the cube by 90° in clockwise direction).

Tip: You can record twists using the «Record» button.

To paint stickers:

(This feature is only available for cubes having 6 different sticker colors.)

  1. Select one of the six colors shown in the toolbar below the cube.
  2. Click at a sticker of the cube.
    (This will paint the selected color.)

Tip: You can paint the cube shown in the «View» tab as well.

Tip: To create a script that solves or generates the current state of the cube, choose «Solve Cube» or «Generate Cube» in the popup menu.
