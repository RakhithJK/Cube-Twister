The Cube Editor - The «Stickers» Tool

The «Stickers» Tool affects the behaviour of the mouse pointer in the 3D view of the cube editor.

When the «Stickers» Tool is selected, then a mouse click on one of the cube stickers will toggle its visible state as well as change the state of the corresponding check box in the «Stickers» tab.
