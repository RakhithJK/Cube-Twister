The Cube Editor - The «Twist» Tool

The «Twist» Tool affects the behaviour of the mouse pointer in the 3D view of the cube editor.

When the «Twist» Tool is selected, then a mouse click on one of the cube stickers will twist a cube layer by 90° in clockwise direction.

Please note: Changes made using the twist tool are not persistent. Every time you open a cube in the cube editor, it is reset to its solved state.
