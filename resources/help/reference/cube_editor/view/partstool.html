The Cube Editor - The «Parts» Tool

The «Parts» Tool affects the behaviour of the mouse pointer in the 3D view of the cube editor.

When the «Parts» Tool is selected, then a mouse click on one of the cube stickers will toggle the visible state of the corresponding cube part as well as change the state of the corresponding check box in the «Parts» tab.
