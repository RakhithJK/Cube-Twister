Removing an Item from the Tree

Please note: You cannot remove a cube or a notation if it is the default. You have to make another cube or another notation the default before you can remove it.

To remove an item from the tree;

  1. Select an item of the tree.
    Open the popup menu of the tree.
  2. Choose «Delete» in the popup menu.
