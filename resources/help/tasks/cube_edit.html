Editing a Cube

To edit a cube:

  1. Open the «Cubes» folder in the tree.
  2. Select a cube.
    (This will show the cube at the right of the tree as well a property sheet further right.)
  3. Use the property sheet to change the properties of the cube
    or
    Click at a tool in the toolbar below the cube, and then click at the cube to apply the tool to the cube.
