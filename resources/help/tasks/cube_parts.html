Showing and Hiding Cube Parts

To show or hide a cube part by clicking at it directly:

  1. Select a cube in the «Cubes» folder of the tree.
    (This will show the cube and a property sheet at the right of the tree.)
  2. Select the «Parts» tool in the toolbar below the cube.
  3. Click at a part of the cube to show or hide it.

To show or hide a cube part by using the property sheet:

  1. Select a cube in the «Cubes» folder of the tree.
    (This will show the cube and a property sheet at the right of the tree.)
  2. Select the «Parts» tab in the property sheet.
  3. Click at the «Visible» check box of the part to show or hide it.
