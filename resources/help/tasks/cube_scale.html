Scaling and Exploding a Cube

To scale or explode a cube:

  1. Select a cube in the «Cubes» folder of the tree.
    (This will show the cube and a property sheet at the right of the tree.)
  2. Choose the «Options» tab in the property sheet.
  3. Choose «Dimension» in the box at the top of the «Options» page.
  4. Use the sliders to adjust the «Scale factor» and the «Explode factor» of the cube.
