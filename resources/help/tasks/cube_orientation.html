Changing the Reset Orientation of a Cube

When you press the «Reset» button in the cube editor or in the script editor, the cube is rotated to its reset orientation.

To change the reset orientation of a cube:

  1. Select a cube in the «Cubes» folder of the tree.
    (This will show the cube and a property sheet at the right of the tree.)
  2. Click the «Reset» button in the property sheet to make sure the cube is in its reset orientation.
  3. Select the «Options» tab in the property sheet.
  4. Choose «Orientation» in the box at the top of the «Options» page.
  5. Use the two sliders to adjust the «Alpha»and the «Beta» angle.
