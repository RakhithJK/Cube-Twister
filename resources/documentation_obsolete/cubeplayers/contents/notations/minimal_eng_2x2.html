Minimal ENG 2x2

Move Right Up Front Left Down Back
○● +90° R U F L D B
○● -90° R' U' F' L' D' B'
○● +180° R2 U2 F2 L2 D2 B2

Minimal ENG 2x2 is a notation for Rubik's Pocket Cube. The notation is based on the English language. It supports 90° and 180° twists of the faces of the cube.

Clockwise 90° twists of the six faces are denoted by the capital letters: «R», «U», «F», «L», «D» and «B», (right, up, front, left, down, back).

Counterclockwise 90° twists are denoted by appending the apostrophe character «'» to a 90° twist: «R'».

180° twists are denoted by appending the number «2» to a 90° twist: «R2».

Werner Randelshofer 2007
